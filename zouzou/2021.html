Marios Evripidou Zouzou

Playful • Joyful • Cherished

Zouzou was born in May 2012, a tiny soul with a gentle spirit. On July 14th of that same year, she was adopted into a world that would become her own. Her life was a journey, woven with quiet moments and playful days, each one a chapter filled with tenderness. Through the years, Zouzou grew, her presence a constant source of comfort, joy, and companionship. Her story came to a close on March 8th, 2024, but her memory, like a soft whisper, lingers on, forever etched in the hearts of those who knew her.

...forever in my heart, eternal in my soul, there's something about you that made me whole...
