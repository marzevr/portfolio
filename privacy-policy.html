Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 01 January 2025
Last Update: 01 August 2025

This website, marios.evripidou.me, is operated by Marios Evripidou. We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with all applicable data protection regulations, including the GDPR.

1. No Personal Data Storage

This is a static website. We do not actively collect, store, or process personal data from visitors.

Any information you voluntarily provide—such as through a contact form or email—is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not stored in a database or shared with third parties.

Since no personal data is stored, requests to access, correct, or delete data are not applicable.

If you reach out via contact form or email, we will only use your provided name and email address to respond to your inquiry. This information is not saved, logged, or used for marketing purposes.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

Pages may contain embedded content (e.g., YouTube videos) or links to third-party websites. Interactions with embedded content are subject to the privacy policies of the third-party services.

We are not responsible for the content or data handling practices of external websites.

5. Data Security

Although we do not process or retain personal data, we take standard precautions to secure any information transmitted through this site (e.g., SSL encryption via HTTPS).

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in legal requirements or site functionality. The effective date will be revised accordingly.

7. Contact
